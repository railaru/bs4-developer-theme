PROJECT:

MT4 Indicator 'HVF Draw Tool'

Find out more ▶

ABOUT:

Built for a private trader client to give his trading an edge, this custom technical indicator designed to draw complex chart patterns faster and with better precision. Just drag the tool on the chart and point out the relative highs/lows. Targets, stoploss, timestops and Fibonacci projections all will be calculated and drawn automatically. If you decide to re-analyse the trading setup you've drawn two years ago and see if extended targets of that setup are still relevant, you're free to do so just by clicking on the indicator menu. To find out more about the theory behind this approach to technical analysis click here.
The tool recognizes wether the setup is bullish or bearish. It then displays the potential risk reward ratio outside the pattern.
Unlimited amount of patterns can be drown on the same chart.
Each drawing can be adjusted dynamically. All derived values derived from the anchor points will be recalculated automatically.
